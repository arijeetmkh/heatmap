What is Conweets?

Ever wanted to know what two tweeps have been tweeting to each other lately or wanted to dig up an old Twitter conversation? Conweets does just that for you!

Simply sign in via Twitter, enter the handles of two users and Conweets will display conversations between them.

Start fetching conversations by clicking the link below


Sign in with Twitter
Why sign in via Twitter?

This helps us make more requests to Twitter, hence giving you better and faster results. We do not post to Twitter on your behalf and we do not reveal any information about who tracks whom.

 

  • How to use Conweets - Step #1

    Click the Sign in with Twitter button which will take you to the Twitter page. Then click Authorize app to proceed

  • How to use Conweets - Step #2

    Next, either use the 'Discover by People' tab to:
    • SINGLE Twitter handle to see who they have been talking to
    • BOTH the handles to view all recent conversations between them.

  • How to use Conweets - Step #2a

    OR,
    Use our new 'Discover by Topic' feature to find people talking about a topic:
    • Simply enter a #hashtag or a search item

  • How to use Conweets - Step #3

    • If you entered a single handle or used 'Discover by Topic', a list of all conversations of that person with others are shown. Click on any of the conversations to view them

  • How to use Conweets - Step #3b

    If you entered both the handles in step 2 or clicked on a particular conversation in the Step 3, you will see the two users' conversations in threaded form
